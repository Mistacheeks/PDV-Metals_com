Investor Resources at PDV Metals
Overview

PDV Metals Limited is a group enterprise specializing in non-ferrous metals, covering the full industrial chain from mining, processing, smelting, to finished product production.


Located in the Lusaka Multi-Facility Economic Zone in Zambia, the steel plant was constructed in 2023 and has a production capacity of 300,000 tons. The plant employs a unique, modern refining process and a comprehensive automated control system to produce high-quality steel, which is extensively used in industrial and civil construction, including highways, bridges, and other high-end areas. The first phase of production is focused on construction rebar, while the second phase will produce angle steel and channel steel.

We are a different kind of steel company. We work everyday to create shareholder value that is:

guided by our policies and focused on securing a sustainable future.

led by our strategy: "well positioned with a high asset portfolio to take advantage of favourable industry and market trends including government incentives.

governed through our Financial Framework that provides a disciplined approach to delivering returns throug hthe cycle, a strong balance sheet and effective capital allocation for long term growth and shareholder returns.

We are based in Lusaka, Zambia and we service most of Central and Southern Africa with our reach spaning at least 6 countries namely Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), Zambia, Malawi, Mozambique, Tanzania and Burundi.

REGIONAL COVERAGE.

The company has built large distribution centers for raw materials and semi-finished products along the transportation routes between South Africa and the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC). The group also operates a large fleet of transport vehicles, enabling it to serve markets across the entire Southern African region

CLIMATE CONSCIOUS MANUFACTURING.

We manufacture and supply steel reinforcement bars for some of the most beautiful and essential buildings and structures, with our products contributing to the African economy in a really majestic way. Our Eco-friendly manufacturing processes set us apart from the competition.
