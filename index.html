About PDV Metals
Your Choice for Quality Rebar Solutions

PDV Metals Limited is a group enterprise specializing in non-ferrous metals, covering the full industrial chain from mining, processing, smelting, to finished product production.

Located in the Lusaka Multi-Facility Economic Zone in Zambia, the steel plant was constructed in 2023 and has a production capacity of 300,000 tons.

The plant employs a unique, modern refining process and a comprehensive automated control system to produce high-quality steel, which is extensively used in industrial and civil construction, including highways, bridges, and other high-end areas. The first phase of production is focused on construction rebar, while the second phase will produce angle steel and channel steel.

The company's workforce of 300 employees is a testament to its commitment to creating jobs and fostering a skilled labor force. PDV Metals envisions a future where it stands as a beacon of industrial excellence in Africa, aspiring to become one of the continent's largest manufacturers and suppliers of quality deformed steel products. This ambition is not just about scale; it's about setting a benchmark for quality and reliability in the steel industry.

The state-of-the-art factory located in the Lusaka Multi-Facility Economic Zone in New Kasama, benefits from the zone's incentives and infrastructure, optimizing PDV Metals' production capabilities.

PDV Metals' focus on producing high-quality deformed steel products is aligned with the growing demand for durable and safe construction materials. By prioritizing excellence and innovation, PDV Metals aims to contribute significantly to the construction sector, which is a vital component of economic development across Zambia and the African continent. The company's growth strategy includes expanding its workforce and enhancing its production processes to meet and exceed industry standards. In doing so, PDV Metals is set to play a pivotal role in Zambia's journey towards industrialization and infrastructural development.

Robust Regional Distribution Network

The company has also built large distribution centers for raw materials and semi-finished products along the transportation routes between South Africa and the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC). The group also operates a large fleet of transport vehicles, enabling it to serve markets across the entire Southern African region.
