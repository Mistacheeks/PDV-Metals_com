A Comprehensive Overview

PDV Metals Limited is a group enterprise specializing in non-ferrous metals, covering the full industrial chain from mining, processing, smelting, to finished product production. Located in the Lusaka Multi-Facility Economic Zone in Zambia, the steel plant was constructed in 2023 and has a production capacity of 300,000 tons. The plant employs a unique, modern refining process and a comprehensive automated control system to produce high-quality steel, which is extensively used in industrial and civil construction, including highways, bridges, and other high-end areas. The first phase of production is focused on construction rebar, while the second phase will produce angle steel and channel steel.

CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY (CSR)
Brief

We care for the communities in which we operate and that is why, as a responsible corporate, PDV Metals Limited is committed to the cause of improving the quality of life of people of all societies in order to contribute to the economic and social development of the periphery and the neighboring communities.

Our policy is directed to ensure that PDV Metals' CSR activities work towards improving the living conditions of the underprivileged and makes a positive difference in their lives.

Environment

At PDV Metals, we truly believe in igniting young minds and in shaping the future of young Africans. Our endeavors are to promote education, including employment enhancing vocational skills especially among children and women and livelihood enhancement projects; monetary contributions to academic institutions for establishing endowment funds, chairs, laboratories, scholarships etc; with the objective of assisting students in their studies.

We seek to ensure environmental sustainability by adopting best ecological practices, support preservation and sensible utilization of natural resources and hence create a sustained positive impact on society. Protection of flora and fauna, animal welfare, agro forestry, conservation of natural resources and maintaining the appropriate quality of soil, air and water.

Rural Development

Strengthening rural areas by improving accessibility, housing, drinking water, sanitation, power and livelihoods, thereby creating sustainability in them.

Direct Influence Zones

Priority would be given to the areas in the immediate vicinity of the plant locations defined as Direct Influence Zone (DIZ). The policy enables plants to define their own DIZ with the provision that this could be expanded as per the size of operations. However, certain programs might be expanded beyond this geographical purview and upscale.
