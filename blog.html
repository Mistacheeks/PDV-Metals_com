A NEW DAWN OF INDUSTRY: Our State-of-the-Art Factory in Lusaka South MFEZ.

A new beacon of industrial innovation is nearing completion within the Lusaka South Multi-Facility Economic Zone (LSMFEZ). We are thrilled to announce that our latest factory, a symbol of progress and potential, is on the brink of opening its doors.

The Vision Comes to Life Our journey began with a vision to contribute to Zambia’s economic diversification and to create a hub that would not only bolster local manufacturing but also shine on the international stage. The LSMFEZ, Zambia’s first state-owned economic zone, has been the perfect cradle for this ambition, offering a mixed-use development space for industrial, commercial, research, and residential purposes

.

A Commitment to Innovation and Excellence Our new factory, which occupies a strategic position within the LSMFEZ, is a testament to our commitment to innovation, quality, and sustainability. Designed to meet the highest standards of production, it stands as a modern facility equipped with cutting-edge technology, ready to serve both local and global markets.

Economic Impact and Job Creation This project is more than just a building; it’s a catalyst for change. With an investment of USD 1.59 billion, the LSMFEZ has already approved 101 investors, and our factory is set to join this growing community. We are proud to be part of an initiative that has created over 31,000 jobs since its inception, with our factory alone expected to add a significant number to this impressive tally.

Looking Ahead As we put the finishing touches on our factory, we look forward to the opportunities it will bring. Not only will it enhance our production capabilities, but it will also allow us to forge stronger connections with other businesses in the zone and beyond. The LSMFEZ’s focus on developing shopping malls, studio apartments, and recreational facilities, including water sports and a golf course, promises a vibrant future for all who work and live in the area.

Join Us on This Exciting Journey We invite you to join us as we embark on this exciting new chapter. Whether you’re a potential employee, a business partner, or a curious observer, we welcome you to witness the growth and success that our new factory will undoubtedly bring to Lusaka and Zambia as a whole.

Stay tuned for more updates as we approach the grand opening of a facility that is not just a factory, but a cornerstone of Zambia’s bright industrial future.
